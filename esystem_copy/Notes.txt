B. Use MYSQL commands to check your relationships

Allowed in your enroll table

1. Enroll a subject to a student.

   ex. insert into enroll values(1,1,1);

Not allowed in your enroll table

1. Enroll a non existing student.

2. Enroll a non existing subject to a student.

3. Enroll the same subject to student Twice.

Allowed in your assign table

1. Assign a subject to a Teacher.

Not allowed in your assign table

1. Assign a subject to a non existing Teacher.

2. Assign a non existing subject to a Teacher.

3. Assign 2 or more teachers to a subject.

Allowed in your grade table

1. grade an existing enrolled subject

Not allowed in your grade table

1. grade a non existing enrolled subject

   ex. insert into grade(1,100,'A','A','A','A'); 100 is a non existing enroll ID

2. grade an enrolled subject twice.

   ex. insert into grade(2,1,'A','A','A','A');insert into grade(3,1,'A','A','A','A');

       grade the same enrolled subject twice.
